IronClaw

Your personal AI agent. Self-hosted, private, always on.

Talk to your AI through WhatsApp. Manage emails, calendar, run deep research, execute commands — all from your phone. Everything runs on your machine. Nothing leaves your device.

Up and running in 2 minutes

Download, install, scan a QR code. That's it.

1

Install the app

Download the DMG (Mac) or EXE (Windows). Drag to Applications and launch. The app runs quietly in your menu bar.

2

Scan the WhatsApp QR

Open WhatsApp on your phone, go to Linked Devices, and scan the QR code shown in the app. IronClaw now lives in your self-chat.

3

Connect your accounts

Optionally link Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, Microsoft 365. All OAuth — your credentials never leave your machine.

4

Start talking

Message yourself on WhatsApp. Ask about your calendar, send emails, research topics, run shell commands, analyze images and PDFs — all from your phone.

What it can do

A growing set of capabilities, all accessible through a simple WhatsApp message.

💬

WhatsApp Interface

No app to learn. Just message yourself on WhatsApp. Text, voice notes, images, PDFs — IronClaw handles them all.

📧

Email Management

Read, send, reply, archive across Gmail and Outlook. "Check my work email" or "reply to John's last message."

📅

Calendar

View upcoming events, create meetings, get morning briefings. Works with Google Calendar and Microsoft 365.

🔬

Deep Research

Multi-agent research via AXON. Explores multiple sources, validates claims, synthesizes comprehensive answers with citations.

👁️

Vision + Documents

Send images and PDFs through WhatsApp. Claude analyzes them with your question. Screenshots, invoices, contracts — anything.

🖥️

Shell + Tasks

Run commands, launch background tasks, manage tmux sessions. Deploy code, run tests, start coding sessions — remotely.

🧠

Memory

Remembers facts about you, your preferences, your projects. Searches past conversations. Gets smarter over time.

🧩

Plugin System

Extensible architecture. Each plugin is a self-contained module. Add new capabilities with a single file.

🔄

Auto-Updates

Detects new versions automatically. Notifies you via WhatsApp. Reply "update" to install — no manual downloads needed.

🔒

Your data stays yours

IronClaw runs entirely on your machine. API keys are stored in your system keychain. The database is encrypted. Nothing is sent to any server except the APIs you explicitly connect. No telemetry, no analytics, no cloud.
